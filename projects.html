📑 Projects📱


Amazon Reviews: ML Text Analysis

What type of Amazon review is most likely to be voted "helpful" by other users? Are there specific textual indicators of a helpful review? I used logistic regression and the bag of words approach to try to answer some of these questions!

NewtBot: Educational Chatbots for LLM Research

I worked at LMU Munich to create a chatbot built on ChatGPT designed to help students learn physics. We continue to investigate how changing our internal priming of the chatbot impacted student educational gain.



Course Matching: Full-Stack App

Course registration for CS majors at Wellesley is notoriously difficult for all parties involved. I worked with a team to design and implement a web application to help students and professors with the process!

Academic Quad: CG & Animation

I worked with a partner to create a 3D rendering of the Academic Quad on campus, modeled after the actual proportions of our favorite spot on campus and complete with seasonal changes and animated camera movement!



Chatrooms: Client-Server Architecture in Networks

I worked with a team to design and implement a client-server based chatroom application that would allow users to chat with any other members in the system in groups of their choosing.

Probing GPT: Investigating Nationality Bias in Occupation Representations

I designed and carried out a probe task to determine if GPT held any inherent implicit biases based on culture and nationality in the occupational space as an indicator of international socioeconomic perception.



Bully Algorithm: Leader Election in Distributed Systems

The original paper describing the Bully algorithm is one of the most famous attempts to solve the widely researched topic of leader election. We used an RPC-based approach to implement the algorithm in Golang.

Budlab: Mobile App Design

Student productivity and socialization took a severe hit during the pandemic. We designed an app that would allow students to work with their peers and find study buddies, even in remote learning!
