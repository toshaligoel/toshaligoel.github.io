🎨 Fine Art 🖌


Acrylic Painting

I love to paint, particularly acrylic painting. Of all the mediums I've tried, acrylic is the most forgiving and accessible. The following works are all a part of a concentration series I did themed around travel. I used several of my own photographs as inspiration for the series, focusing on photorealism as my main artistic style. I ended up creating a 12-piece series that started and ended with images of and around my home, to emulate a sort of "trip around the world" through paintings.

These are a few select pieces from my concentration:


Rainy Day

Based on a photograph I took from a bus window on a rainy London day.

Australian Harbor

Ships I saw coming and going at the Sydney Harbor.

Road Trip

Based on a photograph I took of my sister relaxing en route to a vacation.

Arc De Triomphe

The Arc De Triomphe at sunset, inspired from a trip in 2012.
